Cosmic Talent Tree

Space-themed character progression with animated connections and fog of war

Talent Tree

10 Points Available

Features:

  • 4 Talent Trees: Combat (Red), Tech (Blue), Faith (Purple), Agility (Green)
  • 5 Talents per Tree: Each tree has talents across 3 tiers
  • Animated Connections: Energy pulses flow along connection lines
  • Fog of War: Locked talents are obscured with animated fog effects
  • Space Theme: Starfield background with nebula gradients
  • Interactive: Hover over talents to see tooltips, click to select
