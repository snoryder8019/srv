# Overview

Slab is your all-in-one business platform. It gives you a professional website, client management, invoicing, email marketing, and AI-powered content tools — all under your brand.

## What You Get

- **Your Website** — A fully branded public site with customizable design, blog, portfolio, and custom pages
- **Admin Panel** — Manage everything from one dashboard at `/admin`
- **AI Assistant** — Every content editor has an AI agent that can research and write for you
- **Client Portal** — Clients can log in, view invoices, and collaborate
- **Video Meetings** — Built-in WebRTC meetings with AI notetaking
- **Email Marketing** — Campaigns, contact lists, open/click tracking
- **Invoicing** — Create, send, and track payments via Stripe or PayPal

## How It Works

Your site runs on a subdomain (e.g. `yourbrand.madladslab.com`) or your own custom domain. Everything is isolated — your data, your files, your credentials. No other tenant can see your information.

## Getting Started

1. **Log in** — Visit `/admin/login` and sign in with Google
2. **Set up your brand** — Go to Settings & Keys to fill in your business profile
3. **Customize your site** — Use Design & Settings for colors, fonts, and which sections to show
4. **Add content** — Write blog posts, add portfolio items, edit site copy
5. **Connect integrations** — Add your Stripe, email, and Google credentials in Settings
